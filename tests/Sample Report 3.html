Title: Distillation process

Objective: To separate salt and water from salt solution.

Required materials: Round bottom flask, cork, conical flask, tripod stand, burner, condenser, wire gauze.

Procedure: Salt solution in a round flask was heated. Cold water was passed through the fitted condenser. Distillate was collected in an empty conical flask.

Observation: The solution boiled into steam, passed through the condenser, cooled into water, and collected in the flask. Salt remained in the heating flask.

Result/Conclusion: Solid (solute) and liquid (solvent) can be separated by the distillation process.
