1. Compressibility of Air

Title: Observation of volume of air

Objective: To test whether air has a definite volume or not.

Required materials: Empty mineral water bottle.

Procedure: The lid of the bottle was closed airtight. The bottle was squeezed from bottom to mouth. The lid was opened when it could not be squeezed anymore.

Observation: When the lid was opened, air passed out forcibly.

Result: Air can be compressed.

Conclusion: Air does not have a definite volume and can be compressed.
